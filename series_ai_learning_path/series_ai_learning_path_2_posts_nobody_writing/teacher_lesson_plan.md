# Teacher — Differentiated Lesson Plan

From Post 2 of the Your AI Path series: the example prompt showing what Phase 1 looks like for a teacher.

The problem this solves: a typical lesson plan takes 2–4 hours. This prompt produces a first draft in 90 seconds, freeing up time for actual instruction.

## The prompt

```
I'm teaching a Year 7 science class. Topic: photosynthesis. I have mixed ability levels — some students are advanced readers, some are struggling.

Create a 45-minute lesson plan with differentiated activities for three ability groups, a warm-up question, and a closing quiz.
```

## How to use

Fill in your subject, year/age group, and topic. The example uses Year 7 science and photosynthesis — replace with your own class and topic. Add any other constraints relevant to your situation (school curriculum standards, available equipment, class size).

Review the output and adjust. The model won't know your specific students — the value is the structure and differentiated starting point, not a finished plan.
