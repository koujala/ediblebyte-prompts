# Sales Pro — Pre-Call Preparation

From Post 2 of the Your AI Path series: the example prompt showing what Phase 1 looks like for a sales professional.

The problem this solves: preparation is the edge most salespeople acknowledge matters but consistently skip because it takes too long. Average pre-call prep is under 5 minutes. This prompt delivers 30 minutes of research in 3 minutes, before every call.

## The prompt

```
Here's the LinkedIn profile of the person I'm meeting tomorrow:

[paste LinkedIn bio]

Here's their company's about page:

[paste or summarise company about page]

I sell enterprise project management software. Give me:
1. Three likely pain points based on their role and company size
2. Two questions to open the conversation
3. Likely objections and how to handle them
4. One hook that connects their background to what I offer
```

## How to use

Paste the actual LinkedIn bio and company about page — the more specific the input, the more useful the output. Replace "enterprise project management software" with one clear sentence describing what you sell and who it's for.

Use the output as a starting brief before the call, not a script during it. The pain points and objections are hypotheses — test them, don't assert them.
