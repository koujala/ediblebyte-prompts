# Homemaker — Meal Planning from Existing Ingredients

From Post 2 of the Your AI Path series: the second example prompt for the homemaker role, focused on meal planning from what's already in the house.

The problem this solves: the daily "what's for dinner" decision made under time pressure, often leading to food waste or an unnecessary shopping trip.

## The prompt

```
I have chicken thighs, sweet potato, spinach, garlic, coconut milk, and rice.

What can I make for a family of four this week? Give me three dinners and a lunch using only what I've listed.
```

## How to use

List whatever is actually in your fridge and pantry — the more complete your list, the better the suggestions. Mention family size, dietary restrictions, and how much time you have on weeknights. You can also ask it to factor in a shopping budget or flag ingredients running low.

Iterate: if a suggested meal doesn't appeal, reply with "not that one — what else?" or "replace the coconut milk version with something simpler."
