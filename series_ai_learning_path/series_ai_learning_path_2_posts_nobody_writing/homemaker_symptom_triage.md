# Homemaker — Symptom Triage at Midnight

From Post 2 of the Your AI Path series: the example prompt showing what Phase 1 looks like for a homemaker dealing with a sick child.

The problem this solves: medical decisions landing on one person, often without professional backup at the hours when it's actually needed. This replaces the panic of having no information at midnight — it provides a structured starting point and flags what to raise with a doctor in the morning.

**Note:** This prompt does not replace medical advice. It provides structured information to help you make better decisions about whether and when to seek professional care.

## The prompt

```
My 6-year-old has had a fever of 38.5°C for two days, is eating normally, has a mild cough, and no rash.

What are the likely causes, what should I watch for that would mean going to A&E, and what can I do at home tonight?
```

## How to use

Replace the age, temperature, duration, and symptoms with your child's actual situation. Be specific — the more detail you provide, the more relevant the output. Always follow up with a doctor for anything that concerns you; use this output as a starting point for that conversation, not a substitute for it.
