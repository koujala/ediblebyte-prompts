# AI Engineering JD Review and Rewrite

Audit a job description for an AI engineering role — identify requirements filtering for the wrong candidate profile (ML researchers instead of production engineers), rewrite the JD, and design an interview process that screens for the signals that actually predict success.

```
I am hiring for an AI engineering role with these responsibilities: [describe]. Review this job description: [paste JD]. Identify every requirement that's filtering for the wrong candidate profile, rewrite the JD to accurately describe what the role requires, and design an interview process that screens for the signals that actually predict success.
```

**When to use:** Before posting any AI engineering role. JDs that require ML backgrounds filter out the engineers most likely to ship production AI.
