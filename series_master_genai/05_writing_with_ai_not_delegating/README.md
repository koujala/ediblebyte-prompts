# 05 · Writing With AI — Not Delegating

Prompts from **Article 5** of the Gen AI Mastery series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_master_genai/05_writing_with_ai_not_delegating](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_master_genai/05_writing_with_ai_not_delegating)

**Delegating** hands over the thinking and the voice; the result is competent, structured, and subtly hollow. **Writing with AI** keeps the thinking and voice with you, and uses AI for craft support: first drafts, structure, tone-matching, polish. These prompts are for the second approach.

## Prompts

- [`first_draft_from_brief.md`](first_draft_from_brief.md) — Kill the blank page with a structured first draft you will then critique and rewrite.
- [`write_in_my_voice.md`](write_in_my_voice.md) — Use writing samples to get output that actually sounds like you.
- [`tone_rewrite.md`](tone_rewrite.md) — Recalibrate clarity, tone, and audience fit on existing copy.
- [`difficult_message.md`](difficult_message.md) — Draft honest, direct feedback or bad-news messages without being demoralising.
- [`report_skeleton.md`](report_skeleton.md) — Get the structure before you write a word, so you lead with impact instead of burying it.
