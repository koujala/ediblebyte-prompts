# Prioritise Honestly

AI is a useful forcing function for honest prioritisation — because it doesn't share your avoidance patterns, your sentimental attachment to certain projects, or your tendency to overestimate how long the important work will take.

```
Here is my task list for the week:
[LIST EVERYTHING — don't pre-filter]

For each item, assess:
1. The actual consequence if this doesn't get done this week — is the cost real, or imagined?
2. Whether this is urgent, important, both, or neither.
3. Which items I am probably avoiding, and what the most likely reason for that avoidance is.

Then give me a ranked order for the week, with a one-sentence rationale for each placement.

Be willing to say hard things:
- if something doesn't actually need to happen this week, say so
- if something I listed isn't really mine to do, call it out
- if I'm clearly avoiding one item, name it specifically and suggest the smallest first step that would break the avoidance
```

**The question most people skip:** *"Which items am I avoiding, and why?"* It's also the most revealing. Don't skip it.
