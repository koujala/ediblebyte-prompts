# 06 · AI for Deep Work and Focus

Prompts from **Article 6** of the Gen AI Mastery series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_master_genai/06_ai_for_deep_work_and_focus](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_master_genai/06_ai_for_deep_work_and_focus)

Cognitive load is finite — too many open loops, too many pending decisions, too many unprocessed inputs, and your ability to think deeply collapses. These prompts reduce that load by externalising what's in your head and pre-processing what would otherwise pull at your attention.

> **The rule:** AI earns its keep in the fifteen minutes before your focused block, not during it. Once you start, you start.

## Prompts

- [`morning_plan.md`](morning_plan.md) — Start the day with intention instead of reacting to your inbox.
- [`brain_dump_sort.md`](brain_dump_sort.md) — Empty your head onto the page; let AI sort what to act on, defer, or consciously let go of.
- [`prioritise_honestly.md`](prioritise_honestly.md) — A forcing function for honest prioritisation, including what you're avoiding.
- [`pre_meeting_prep.md`](pre_meeting_prep.md) — Walk into a meeting clear on what you need to land and what you need to leave with.
- [`end_of_day_shutdown.md`](end_of_day_shutdown.md) — Close the mental tabs so tomorrow has a starting point and your evening is yours.
