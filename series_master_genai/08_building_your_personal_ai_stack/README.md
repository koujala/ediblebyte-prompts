# 08 · Building Your Personal AI Stack

Prompts from **Article 8** of the Gen AI Mastery series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_master_genai/08_building_your_personal_ai_stack](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_master_genai/08_building_your_personal_ai_stack)

A stack without a system is just a collection. Three layers make it a system: a **core tool**, one or two **specialists**, and — the layer most people skip — the **habits and prompts** that connect tools to tasks you actually do. These prompts help you build that third layer deliberately.

## Prompts

- [`stack_audit.md`](stack_audit.md) — A 90-day self-assessment of whether your current stack is producing real value, or just occupying time.
- [`prompt_library_starter.md`](prompt_library_starter.md) — Turn your five most recurring tasks into reusable prompts you'll actually use.
- [`habit_triggers.md`](habit_triggers.md) — Attach AI prompts to moments that already exist in your day, so using them doesn't require discipline.
