# 04 · AI for Learning Anything Faster

Prompts from **Article 4** of the Gen AI Mastery series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_master_genai/04_ai_for_learning_faster](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_master_genai/04_ai_for_learning_faster)

AI doesn't make you a better learner by doing the learning for you — it removes every friction that used to make you stop. These prompts turn AI into a patient, adjustable tutor: one that explains at your level, pushes back on fuzzy thinking, fills real-time gaps, and quizzes you in a way a static textbook never can.

## Prompts

- [`adjustable_tutor.md`](adjustable_tutor.md) — Get an explanation of any concept, then turn the complexity dial up or down as needed.
- [`feynman_explain_back.md`](feynman_explain_back.md) — Explain a concept to the AI as a demanding student; let it point to every crack.
- [`learning_plan.md`](learning_plan.md) — Build a sequenced, milestone-based plan around your real time and learning style.
- [`gap_filler_and_quiz.md`](gap_filler_and_quiz.md) — Resolve a specific mid-reading confusion, and use spaced-repetition quizzing to make it stick.
