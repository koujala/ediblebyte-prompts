# 01 · What Is Generative AI — Really?

Prompts from **Article 1** of the Gen AI Mastery series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_master_genai/01_what_is_gen_ai](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_master_genai/01_what_is_gen_ai)

The article strips away hype and fear and explains what AI actually is: pattern recognition at scale, with a fixed knowledge cutoff, no real-time awareness, and no guarantee of factual accuracy. These prompts help you build direct, personal intuition for how the model actually behaves.

## Prompts

- [`observe_the_model.md`](observe_the_model.md) — Test the model on a topic you know deeply and a recent event, side by side, to see where fluency and accuracy diverge.
- [`knowledge_self_check.md`](knowledge_self_check.md) — Use AI to surface what you know deeply vs. what you are merely confident about on a given topic.
