# 09 · The Mindset Shift — User to Practitioner

Prompts from **Article 9** of the Gen AI Mastery series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_master_genai/09_mindset_shift_user_to_practitioner](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_master_genai/09_mindset_shift_user_to_practitioner)

A **user** reacts to AI. A **practitioner** thinks with AI — they iterate rather than accept, bring judgment to every output, and know where AI belongs and where it does not. The difference compounds over time. These closing prompts are about defining what practitioner-level mastery looks like for *you specifically*, and building the reflection loop that sharpens it.

## Prompts

- [`define_my_mastery.md`](define_my_mastery.md) — Name what meaningful AI mastery looks like in your specific work and life, and the single 90-day focus that will get you there.
- [`intuition_log.md`](intuition_log.md) — A reflection prompt for building AI intuition from the prompts that worked brilliantly and the ones that disappointed.
