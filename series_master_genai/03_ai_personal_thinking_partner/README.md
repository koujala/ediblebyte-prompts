# 03 · AI as Your Personal Thinking Partner

Prompts from **Article 3** of the Gen AI Mastery series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_master_genai/03_ai_personal_thinking_partner](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_master_genai/03_ai_personal_thinking_partner)

AI is a sounding board, not a decision-maker. Used well, it helps you *open decisions up before you close them down* — surfacing what you are missing, challenging what you are assuming, asking the questions you should be sitting with. You stay in the driving seat; your thinking just has more to work with.

## Prompts

- [`steelman_decision.md`](steelman_decision.md) — Stress-test a decision by asking AI to make the strongest possible case for the option you are leaning *away* from.
- [`questions_first.md`](questions_first.md) — For big decisions: ask for the right questions to sit with, not answers.
- [`goal_discovery.md`](goal_discovery.md) — Surface what you actually value before committing to 90-day goals.
- [`annual_review.md`](annual_review.md) — An honest, AI-assisted review across career, relationships, health, and personal growth.
