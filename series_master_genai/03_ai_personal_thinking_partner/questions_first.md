# Questions First, Not Answers

For the decisions that actually matter, the most valuable thing AI can give you is not a recommendation — it's the right questions to sit with. This prompt explicitly refuses AI's advice so you get the thinking prompts instead.

```
I am sitting with a significant decision and I do not want your answer — I want the right questions.

Here is the situation in full:
[DESCRIBE HONESTLY — what the decision is, what is at stake, what you are afraid of, what you are hoping for, what you cannot change, what you have tried so far]

Do not tell me what to do. Do not tell me the pros and cons. Do not recommend an option.

Instead, give me:
1. The five questions I most need to sit with before making this decision — the kind that reveal what I actually value and what I am avoiding.
2. One question that a person I deeply respect would ask me, if they knew the full situation.
3. One question I am probably avoiding because I already suspect the answer.

Keep each question sharp, specific to my situation, and open — no yes/no questions, no leading questions.
```

**How to use the output:** Close the laptop. Sit with the questions for at least 24 hours before you come back. The decision is preparation for the questions, not the other way around.
