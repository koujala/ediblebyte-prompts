# 07 · AI at Work — Without the Overwhelm

Prompts from **Article 7** of the Gen AI Mastery series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_master_genai/07_ai_at_work_without_the_overwhelm](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_master_genai/07_ai_at_work_without_the_overwhelm)

You weren't hired for your ability to write status updates, format reports, or produce meeting summaries. You were hired for your judgment, relationships, and ability to navigate complexity. These prompts hand the operational overhead to AI so your working hours can go to work that's actually worthy of what you bring.

> **The mapping rule:** If you could write a precise enough description of a task that someone else could complete it without asking a single follow-up question — AI can handle it.

## Prompts

- [`meeting_follow_up.md`](meeting_follow_up.md) — Turn rough meeting notes into a clean follow-up with decisions, actions, and open questions.
- [`research_synthesis.md`](research_synthesis.md) — Orient quickly on an unfamiliar topic, and synthesise scattered notes into a coherent narrative.
- [`decision_frame.md`](decision_frame.md) — Get the structure, trade-offs, and missing information for a hard call — without handing over the call itself.
- [`stakeholder_update.md`](stakeholder_update.md) — A stakeholder message calibrated to what matters to *them*, not what's most recent for you.
