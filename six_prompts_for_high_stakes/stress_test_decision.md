# Stress-Test a Decision Before the Crowd Gets to It

You're leaning toward a direction. Hear the strongest counter-argument before the room pushes back and you're defending instead of evaluating.

```
I'm considering [decision]. Find the real weaknesses, not surface ones. Don't validate me. Tell me what a thoughtful critic with actual skin in the game would say.
```

**When to use:** Before finalising any decision that will be hard to reverse — architecture choices, hiring calls, pricing changes, partnership commitments.
