# 6 Prompts for High-Stakes Moments

Six prompts for the moments where knowledge alone isn't enough — decisions that need pressure-testing, feedback you're not sure how to read, negotiations where silence would have won.

## Prompts

- [`clear_assumptions.md`](clear_assumptions.md) — Strip your assumptions before walking into a high-stakes situation
- [`find_understanding_gaps.md`](find_understanding_gaps.md) — Surface where your understanding breaks before someone else does
- [`stress_test_decision.md`](stress_test_decision.md) — Hear the strongest counter-argument before the room does
- [`separate_feedback_from_noise.md`](separate_feedback_from_noise.md) — Weigh feedback without dismissing or overreacting
- [`strategic_silence.md`](strategic_silence.md) — Train the reflex to hold silence in negotiation
- [`one_surprise_rule.md`](one_surprise_rule.md) — Close every session with the one thing worth carrying forward

## Usage

Paste any prompt into Claude or ChatGPT. Replace `[brackets]` with your specifics. Each prompt is designed for a specific moment — use the one that matches where you are.
