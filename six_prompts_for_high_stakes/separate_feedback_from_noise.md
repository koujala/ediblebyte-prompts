# Separate Feedback From Noise

Someone gave you feedback. It stung, or it felt off, or you're not sure if it matters. Neither dismissing nor overreacting helps.

```
Someone gave me this feedback: [feedback]. Is this a fundamental issue or a surface preference? What would someone with deep expertise actually challenge here?
```

**When to use:** After any review, retro, or 1:1 where you received feedback you're not sure how to weigh.
