# Clear Your Assumptions First

Before walking into a situation you think you understand — a client problem, team conflict, technical decision. Your past experience might be making you skip steps.

```
Treat me as a complete beginner on [situation]. Don't assume I'm reading it correctly. When my framing is off, tell me directly and explain what I'm missing.
```

**When to use:** Before any high-stakes conversation where your experience might be making you skip steps.
