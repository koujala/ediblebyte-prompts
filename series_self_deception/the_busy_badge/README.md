# The Busy Badge

Prompts from **The Busy Badge** in the Self-Deception Series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_self_deception/the_busy_badge](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_self_deception/the_busy_badge)

The article applies Goodhart's Law personally: once busyness became the metric, people optimise for busyness. High activity, slow progress. Neither the person nor those around them notice, because the activity is real. These prompts create the honest accounting that busyness cannot survive.

## Prompts

- [`weekly_output_audit.md`](weekly_output_audit.md) — Ask AI to evaluate your week against the goals that actually matter, not the calendar that looked full.
- [`design_outcome_week.md`](design_outcome_week.md) — Ask AI to help structure a week where success is defined by what moved, not what was attended.
