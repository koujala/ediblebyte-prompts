# The Almost Trap

Prompts from **The Almost Trap** in the Self-Deception Series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_self_deception/the_almost_trap](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_self_deception/the_almost_trap)

The article distinguishes preparation from protection: preparation gets you closer to a specific, concrete outcome; protection delays the moment of being evaluated. Both feel like work. Only one of them moves anything. These prompts get an honest external read on which one is happening.

## Prompts

- [`test_ready_or_safe.md`](test_ready_or_safe.md) — Ask AI to evaluate your work against the actual standard, not against your fear of shipping.
- [`identify_real_threshold.md`](identify_real_threshold.md) — Ask AI to examine each blocker and determine whether it is a genuine quality gap or a protection mechanism.
