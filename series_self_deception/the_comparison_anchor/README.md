# The Comparison Anchor

Prompts from **The Comparison Anchor** in the Self-Deception Series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_self_deception/the_comparison_anchor](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_self_deception/the_comparison_anchor)

The article identifies three compounding biases: Anchoring (the first reference point dominates all evaluation), the Availability Heuristic (the most successful person in your peer group is the most memorable), and Survivorship Bias (we see the people who succeeded; the rest are invisible). The wrong reference class produces wrong conclusions. These prompts replace the single anecdote with a more honest picture.

## Prompts

- [`audit_comparison_anchor.md`](audit_comparison_anchor.md) — Ask AI to examine the invisible inputs in the outcome you are anchored to.
- [`build_right_reference_class.md`](build_right_reference_class.md) — Ask AI to construct a realistic reference class for your actual situation, rather than the outlier you are measuring against.
