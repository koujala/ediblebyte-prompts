# Force the Action Call

Use this prompt when you have gathered significant information about a decision but have not acted on it. The prompt asks AI to make the call you keep deferring, and to cost out what continued analysis is actually worth.

The article frames overthinking as self-protective: staying in analysis means the outcome is still undetermined, which means it cannot be evaluated, which means you remain unaccountable. This prompt removes that shelter.

---

## Prompt

```
Here is what I know about [DECISION / SITUATION]: [SUMMARISE YOUR RESEARCH AND ANALYSIS SO FAR].

Is there any information I'm missing that would genuinely change the outcome? Or is this enough to act — and what would one more week of analysis actually add?
```

---

**How to use:** Summarise what you actually know — not what you plan to find out. Keep the summary honest. Then read the response and notice whether the answer surprises you or confirms what you already knew.

**What to watch for:** If you already know the answer before you hit send, that is the tell the article describes. The things people are genuinely stuck on: they do not know the next step. The things people are overthinking: they know exactly what it is.
