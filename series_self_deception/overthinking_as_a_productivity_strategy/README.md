# Overthinking as a Productivity Strategy

Prompts from **Overthinking as a Productivity Strategy** in the Self-Deception Series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_self_deception/overthinking_as_a_productivity_strategy](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_self_deception/overthinking_as_a_productivity_strategy)

The article identifies the moment when analysis stops adding clarity and starts adding delay with extra steps. Overthinking is not the inability to decide — it is the decision to keep deciding, because preparation has no downside risk. This prompt forces the call.

## Prompts

- [`force_the_action_call.md`](force_the_action_call.md) — Ask AI whether you already have enough to act, and what one more week of analysis would actually add.
