# The Resurrection Trap

Prompts from **The Resurrection Trap** in the Self-Deception Series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_self_deception/the_resurrection_trap](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_self_deception/the_resurrection_trap)

The article identifies the trap not as the needs-improvement rating the rescuer receives, but as answering the call in the first place. Every answered call teaches the organisation that crises can be generated, rescuers can be found, and the cost will be paid by someone other than the people who made the decisions that caused it. This prompt maps the accountability before the answer is given.

## Prompts

- [`map_crisis_accountability.md`](map_crisis_accountability.md) — Ask AI to trace who owns the decisions that created the crisis, and who learns the lesson if you fix it.
