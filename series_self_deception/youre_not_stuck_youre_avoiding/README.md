# You're Not Stuck. You're Avoiding.

Prompts from **You're Not Stuck. You're Avoiding.** in the Self-Deception Series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_self_deception/youre_not_stuck_youre_avoiding](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_self_deception/youre_not_stuck_youre_avoiding)

The article draws a sharp distinction: the things people are truly stuck on — they do not know the next step. The things people are avoiding — they know exactly what the next step is. The busyness filling the stuck period is not accidental; it is the mind's preferred alternative to the thing it is not doing. This prompt reflects the pattern back from the outside.

## Prompts

- [`name_what_youre_avoiding.md`](name_what_youre_avoiding.md) — Ask AI to describe what someone avoiding the real next step would look like, then compare it to your situation.
