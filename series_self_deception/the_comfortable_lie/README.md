# The Comfortable Lie

Prompts from **The Comfortable Lie** in the Self-Deception Series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_self_deception/the_comfortable_lie](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_self_deception/the_comfortable_lie)

The article identifies post-hoc rationalisation: the decision comes first, driven by instinct or desire; the reasoning comes second, assembled to justify what was already chosen. The reasoning feels like the cause. It is the alibi. These two prompts do different work — one tests the direction, one tests the process.

## Prompts

- [`scratch_test.md`](scratch_test.md) — Ask AI what it would recommend if you were starting fresh today, with no prior commitment.
- [`tell_finder.md`](tell_finder.md) — Ask AI to examine the sequence of your decision for signs that the conclusion preceded the analysis.
