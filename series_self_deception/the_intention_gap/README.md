# The Intention Gap

Prompts from **The Intention Gap** in the Self-Deception Series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_self_deception/the_intention_gap](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_self_deception/the_intention_gap)

The article identifies a structural asymmetry: we judge ourselves by what we intended; everyone else judges us by what we did. The Spotlight Effect compounds it — we believe our good intention is visible to others. It is not. These prompts provide the external read that social courtesy makes rare.

## Prompts

- [`read_behaviour_from_outside.md`](read_behaviour_from_outside.md) — Ask AI how your behaviour reads to others, with the intention removed from the frame.
- [`close_the_loop_on_pattern.md`](close_the_loop_on_pattern.md) — Ask AI what a recurring behaviour looks like from the other side, and what it would take for the behaviour to match the intention.
