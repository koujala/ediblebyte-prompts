# The Hindsight Ratchet

Prompts from **The Hindsight Ratchet** in the Self-Deception Series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_self_deception/the_hindsight_ratchet](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_self_deception/the_hindsight_ratchet)

The article identifies the ratchet: after an outcome is known, we rewrite our prior uncertainty as prior knowledge. Good outcomes are absorbed into the model as evidence of judgment; bad outcomes are explained by circumstances. The result is a completely distorted model of our own decision quality. These prompts use AI as a timestamped record that memory cannot revise.

## Prompts

- [`record_uncertainty_before_outcome.md`](record_uncertainty_before_outcome.md) — Record your actual state of mind before the outcome is known, so you cannot revise it later.
- [`audit_past_decision_for_hindsight.md`](audit_past_decision_for_hindsight.md) — Examine a past decision to find the gap between what you actually knew and what you now believe you knew.
