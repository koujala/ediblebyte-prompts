# Confidence Isn't Competence

Prompts from **Confidence Isn't Competence** in the Self-Deception Series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_self_deception/confidence_isnt_competence](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_self_deception/confidence_isnt_competence)

The article examines the most dangerous phase of learning — when enough has been acquired to feel capable, but not enough to know how much is still missing. The vocabulary is there; the judgment isn't. This prompt surfaces the gap.

## Prompts

- [`map_competence_gap.md`](map_competence_gap.md) — Ask AI what genuine competence actually requires at your level, and what mistakes someone there typically makes without noticing.
