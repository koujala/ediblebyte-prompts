# Post 1 · Honest Starting Point: The Map, the Stakes, and Why This Series Exists

Prompts from **Article 1** of the Enterprise Agentic AI: A Practitioner's Guide series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_agentic_ai_guide/series_agentic_ai_guide_1_honest_starting_point](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_agentic_ai_guide/series_agentic_ai_guide_1_honest_starting_point)

This article establishes the core mental model: enterprise agentic AI is a technical problem wrapped inside a political one. Non-determinism changes everything about how you build, deploy, and operate agents. The organizations winning in 2026 are the ones who shipped something narrow, measured it honestly, and used that evidence as political capital.

## Prompts

This post is foundational framing — it establishes the mental model and philosophy but does not contain standalone reusable prompt templates. The actionable tools begin in Post 2.
