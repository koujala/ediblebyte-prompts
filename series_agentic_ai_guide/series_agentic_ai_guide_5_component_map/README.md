# Post 5 · The Component Map and Navigating Your Stakeholders

Prompts from **Article 5** of the Enterprise Agentic AI: A Practitioner's Guide series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_agentic_ai_guide/series_agentic_ai_guide_5_component_map](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_agentic_ai_guide/series_agentic_ai_guide_5_component_map)

The technology is the easier part. This post gives the full component map at a glance and a frank look at the organizational reality that determines whether any of it ships. The teams that succeed at enterprise agentic AI are not the ones with the most sophisticated prompts — they are the ones with the clearest documentation, the most consistent communication, and the discipline to ship incrementally.

## Prompts

- [`stakeholder_conversation_prep.md`](stakeholder_conversation_prep.md) — Prepare specific, document-backed responses to the three predictable stakeholder conversations: security, compliance, and business value. Vague concerns evaporate when you're specific; they compound when you respond with equal vagueness.
