## Prompts

This post is the series close — it names the principle and the discipline without introducing new frameworks or templates. The reusable tools are in the earlier posts.

The one thing to carry: **The sophistication comes later. The discipline has to come first.**

If you are three months in and the architecture feels insufficient, when stakeholders are asking for capabilities you haven't built yet, when a competitor announces something flashy — that is the moment to come back to the prompts in Posts 2–5 and ask: is the foundation trustworthy? If yes, you are already ahead of most.
