# Post 11 · The Scientist & Researcher

Prompts from **Post 11** of the AI By Role series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_ai_by_role/series_ai_by_role_11_the_scientist_and_researcher](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_ai_by_role/series_ai_by_role_11_the_scientist_and_researcher)

The article's central idea: scientists spend 40–60% of non-lab time on communication and documentation overhead. AI doesn't do science. It handles the communication layer around it — literature synthesis, grant writing, methods sections, lay summaries. The discovery layer stays human.
