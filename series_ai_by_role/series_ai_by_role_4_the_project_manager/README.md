# Post 4 · The Project Manager

Prompts from **Post 4** of the AI By Role series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_ai_by_role/series_ai_by_role_4_the_project_manager](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_ai_by_role/series_ai_by_role_4_the_project_manager)

The article's central idea: status reports, meeting notes, and stakeholder updates are the documentation of project management — not the actual job. They consume the majority of the PM workweek. AI handles the translation from raw notes to structured output. The time that frees up goes to decisions, unblocking, and risk management.
