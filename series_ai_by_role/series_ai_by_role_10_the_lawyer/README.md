# Post 10 · The Lawyer

Prompts from **Post 10** of the AI By Role series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_ai_by_role/series_ai_by_role_10_the_lawyer](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_ai_by_role/series_ai_by_role_10_the_lawyer)

The article's central idea: drafting a standard NDA used to take 90 minutes — now it takes 15. AI doesn't have legal judgment. It does have the ability to produce a first-pass draft of almost any standard legal document in seconds. That's not a threat to the profession — it's a reallocation of where expertise goes.

**Critical rule for all prompts in this folder:** Never input sensitive client data, privileged communications, or identifiable case details into a public frontier model. Work with anonymised or hypothetical parameters when generating drafts, then apply specific facts manually in an approved environment.
