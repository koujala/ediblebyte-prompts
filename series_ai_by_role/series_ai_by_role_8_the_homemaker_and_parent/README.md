# Post 8 · The Homemaker & Parent

Prompts from **Post 8** of the AI By Role series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_ai_by_role/series_ai_by_role_8_the_homemaker_and_parent](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_ai_by_role/series_ai_by_role_8_the_homemaker_and_parent)

The article's central idea: the AI content world writes for professionals with job titles. The homemaker and parent managing a household, raising children, navigating medical decisions, and doing all of it largely alone is the most underserved audience in the entire AI conversation. The wins are immediate, practical, and often more meaningful than anything in a productivity post.

## Prompts

- [`fridge_meal_plan.md`](fridge_meal_plan.md) — Generate dinners and packed lunches from whatever is already in the house, with family constraints built in.
- [`child_symptom_triage.md`](child_symptom_triage.md) — Get a structured, plain-English assessment of a child's symptoms to prepare for after-hours decisions and morning GP calls.
