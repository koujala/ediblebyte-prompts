# Post 13 · The Healthcare Professional

Prompts from **Post 13** of the AI By Role series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_ai_by_role/series_ai_by_role_13_the_healthcare_professional](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_ai_by_role/series_ai_by_role_13_the_healthcare_professional)

The article's central idea: physicians and nurses spend 34–55% of their working hours on documentation — not patient care. AI handles the communication and documentation layer around clinical work. The clinical judgment remains non-negotiable, always.

**Critical rule for all prompts in this folder:** No patient data, no identifiable information, no clinical details that could link to a real person goes into a public frontier model. Ever. This is a non-negotiable professional and legal boundary. All prompts here are designed to work without patient data — the clinical knowledge is yours; the AI helps you communicate it.

## Prompts

- [`soap_note_structure.md`](soap_note_structure.md) — Structure a clinical encounter note into SOAP format — for use only in an organisation-approved, compliant environment.
- [`patient_explanation.md`](patient_explanation.md) — Draft a plain-English explanation of a diagnosis, procedure, or treatment plan for a patient or family member.
