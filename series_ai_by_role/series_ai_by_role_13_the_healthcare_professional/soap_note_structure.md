# SOAP Note Structure

Use this to format a clinical encounter into a structured SOAP note — reducing documentation time between patients rather than batching it at the end of a shift.

**This prompt is for use only in an organisation-approved, compliant environment with appropriate data governance in place. Do not use a public consumer AI tool for any prompt that includes actual patient details.**

---

```
I'm going to describe a patient encounter. Create a structured SOAP note from what I describe.

As you format it:
- Keep clinical language accurate and appropriate for a [SPECIALTY — e.g., general practice / emergency medicine / paediatrics] setting
- Flag anything that seems ambiguous or that needs clarification before I sign
- Do not add clinical details I haven't stated — only structure what I give you

Here's the encounter: [DICTATE OR TYPE THE ENCOUNTER DETAILS — in your approved environment only]
```

---

**How to use this:** Every note this produces must be reviewed, validated, and signed by the clinician responsible for that patient's care before it is finalised or shared. The model structures what you give it — your clinical expertise validates it. There is no automated clinical decision in this framework.

**Know your environment:** Confirm your organisation's AI policy before using any tool for anything that touches patient care. The tools approved for clinical documentation in your setting — and the workflow required for clinician sign-off — are determined by your organisation's information governance process, not by individual preference.
