# Post 7 · The Student

Prompts from **Post 7** of the AI By Role series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_ai_by_role/series_ai_by_role_7_the_student](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_ai_by_role/series_ai_by_role_7_the_student)

The article's central idea: there are two ways to use AI as a student. One produces an answer you submit without reading — the gap compounds and the exam is unforgiving. The other produces understanding you couldn't have built as fast without it. AI as a tutor available at 2am, infinitely patient, that can always rephrase until the concept lands.

- [`socratic_concept_explainer.md`](socratic_concept_explainer.md) — Get a concept explained clearly in plain language, then tested back to you to confirm you actually understood it.
