# Pre-Call Research Brief

Use this before every sales call. Paste in the prospect's LinkedIn bio and company page summary — get a personalised research brief in under 2 minutes. You walk in more prepared than the competitor who didn't bother.

---

```
Here's the LinkedIn profile of the person I'm meeting [tomorrow / on [DATE]]: [PASTE BIO OR KEY PROFILE DETAILS].

Here's their company's about page: [PASTE OR SUMMARISE THE COMPANY — industry, size, what they do].

I sell [YOUR PRODUCT OR SERVICE — one sentence].

Give me:
1. Three likely pain points based on their role and company size.
2. Two opening questions that show I've done my homework.
3. The two objections I'm most likely to hear and how to handle them.
4. One angle that connects their background to what I offer.
```

---

**How to use this:** Run this before every call — not just the important ones. You know you're using it right when it's automatic, not occasional. Read the brief; don't read from it in the call. Add the one detail only you would know from your prior relationship or any additional research.

**Extend it for:** Personalised outreach emails (share the brief and ask: "Now write a cold outreach email using this context — under 150 words, no generic opening"), follow-up sequences (describe what was discussed in the actual call and ask for a follow-up that references the real conversation), proposal first drafts (describe the prospect's situation and your proposed solution in three bullet points).
