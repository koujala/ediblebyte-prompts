# Post 12 · The Creative

Prompts from **Post 12** of the AI By Role series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_ai_by_role/series_ai_by_role_12_the_creative](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_ai_by_role/series_ai_by_role_12_the_creative)

The article's central idea: AI doesn't have taste. You do. That's still the whole job — AI just killed the blank page. The creative's value is judgment: knowing what will resonate, what will stop someone mid-scroll, what idea is genuinely interesting versus competent and forgettable. AI removes the blank page problem and the administrative layer.
