# Post 2 · The Teacher

Prompts from **Post 2** of the AI By Role series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_ai_by_role/series_ai_by_role_2_the_teacher](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_ai_by_role/series_ai_by_role_2_the_teacher)

The article's central idea: 74% of teachers spend more time on administrative work than on actual instruction. AI doesn't change what you know about your students or your subject — it changes what you have to do before you can teach them. A lesson plan that used to take 3 hours takes 90 seconds to draft.
