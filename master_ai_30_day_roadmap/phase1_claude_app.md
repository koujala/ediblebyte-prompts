# Phase 1 — Claude App: Learn & Produce (Days 1–14)

Day-by-day prompts for building deep understanding of a topic, producing content, and capturing what you've learned.

---

## Day 1 — Choose your topic and let Claude interview you

```
I want to spend the next two weeks building a deep understanding of [topic]. I'm starting at [beginner / some knowledge / experienced but patchy]. Before we dive in, ask me 5 questions that will help you understand what I already know and what gaps I most need to fill.
```

---

## Days 2–3 — Build your foundation

```
Based on what I told you, give me a structured learning map for [topic]. Break it into 4–6 core areas. For each area, give me: (1) the key concept in plain language, (2) why it matters, (3) one common misconception people have about it.
```

**Follow-up when something doesn't click:**
```
You mentioned [concept]. I don't fully understand [specific part]. Explain it differently — use an analogy from [your field / everyday life].
```

---

## Days 4–5 — Go deeper on what matters most

```
I want to go deeper on [sub-topic within your domain]. Teach me the nuance — what do most beginners get wrong? What does someone who really understands this think differently about compared to someone who only knows the surface?
```

**Challenge prompt:**
```
Give me 3 scenarios where [concept] plays out differently than most people expect. Walk me through each one.
```

---

## Day 6 — Quiz yourself

```
Quiz me on what we've covered about [topic]. Ask me 5 questions — mix of factual recall and application. After I answer each one, tell me what I got right, what I missed, and what the fuller answer looks like.
```

## Day 7 — Apply to a real scenario

```
Give me a realistic scenario in [your context — your job, your project, your life]. Ask me to apply what I've learned about [topic] to that scenario. Then critique my response.
```

---

## Days 8–9 — Connect to your real world

```
I work in [role / industry] and I'm trying to [goal or challenge]. Based on what I now know about [topic], what are the 3 most relevant things I should be doing differently? Be specific — not general advice, but things I can act on this week.
```

**Follow-up:**
```
For [the most relevant suggestion], walk me through exactly how I'd start. What's the first action, what do I do next, and what should I watch out for?
```

---

## Days 10–11 — Produce content

**Day 10 — generate angles:**
```
Based on everything I've learned about [topic], suggest 5 content angles I could write about. For each one: the audience it's best for, the core idea in one sentence, and why it would be useful.
```

**Day 11 — draft:**
```
I want to write a [post / article / guide] about [chosen angle]. Here are my rough thoughts: [paste 3–5 sentences of your raw ideas]. Help me structure this into a clear outline — then write a first draft I can edit. Keep my voice — direct, no fluff.
```

---

## Day 12 — Capture your knowledge snapshot

```
Summarise everything we've covered about [topic] over the past two weeks. Organise it into: (1) core concepts I now understand, (2) key mental models, (3) things I should revisit or go deeper on, (4) how this applies to my situation.
```

## Day 13 — Build your prompt library

```
What are the 10 most important prompts someone studying [topic] should keep? Write them as reusable templates with [brackets] for the parts to customise.
```

---

## Day 14 — Retrieval review

```
Ask me to walk you through what I learned this week. Then ask me follow-up questions to probe whether I actually understand it or just remember the words.
```
