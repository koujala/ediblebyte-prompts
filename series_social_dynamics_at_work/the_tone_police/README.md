# The Tone Police

Prompts from this article in the Social Dynamics at Work series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_social_dynamics_at_work/the_tone_police](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_social_dynamics_at_work/the_tone_police)

The article examines tone critique deployed as the first response to uncomfortable content — how it redirects a hard conversation toward a safer one and teaches the team that silence is safer next time. AI receives blunt, unfiltered input and evaluates the substance without social friction.

## Prompts

- [`test_substance_before_the_room.md`](test_substance_before_the_room.md) — Verify that a concern holds up substantively and reframe it to make the content harder to dismiss.
- [`extract_signal_from_delivery.md`](extract_signal_from_delivery.md) — Separate the actual concern from how it was expressed when something was dismissed for tone.
