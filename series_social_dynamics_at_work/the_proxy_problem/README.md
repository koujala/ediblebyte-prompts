# The Proxy Problem

Prompts from this article in the Social Dynamics at Work series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_social_dynamics_at_work/the_proxy_problem](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_social_dynamics_at_work/the_proxy_problem)

The article describes Goodhart's Law in the workplace: when a measure becomes a target, it stops being a good measure. The person who optimises for actual outcomes gets a yellow flag; the person who optimises for visible activity gets "exceeds expectations." This is a measurement design problem, not a people problem.
