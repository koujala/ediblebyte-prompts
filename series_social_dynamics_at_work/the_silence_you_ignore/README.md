# The Silence You Ignore

Prompts from this article in the Social Dynamics at Work series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_social_dynamics_at_work/the_silence_you_ignore](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_social_dynamics_at_work/the_silence_you_ignore)

The article argues that the felt sense — the quiet signal that something is off before the justifications arrive — is not mysticism. It is pattern recognition assembled from experience, operating faster than conscious analysis. Most people have learned to override it efficiently. This prompt helps find the cause rather than dismiss the signal.

## Prompts

- [`investigate_the_felt_sense.md`](investigate_the_felt_sense.md) — Find the likely cause of a strong gut sense of wrongness instead of overriding it with rational arguments for proceeding.
