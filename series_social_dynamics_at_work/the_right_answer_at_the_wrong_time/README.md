# The Right Answer at the Wrong Time

Prompts from this article in the Social Dynamics at Work series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_social_dynamics_at_work/the_right_answer_at_the_wrong_time](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_social_dynamics_at_work/the_right_answer_at_the_wrong_time)

The article argues that advice lands when the recipient has enough context to make it meaningful — and before that point, the most accurate advice is just noise with good intentions. Readiness is not about intelligence; it is about where someone is in the cycle of their own understanding. Some things cannot be accelerated — they require the experience that makes them receivable.

- [`diagnose_learning_readiness.md`](diagnose_learning_readiness.md) — Identify what needs to be true before a concept or skill can actually land — including whether the next step is study or lived experience.
