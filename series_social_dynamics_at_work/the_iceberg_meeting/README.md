# The Iceberg Meeting

Prompts from this article in the Social Dynamics at Work series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_social_dynamics_at_work/the_iceberg_meeting](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_social_dynamics_at_work/the_iceberg_meeting)

The article describes the moment when one person in a confident room knows what is underneath the diagram — and the choice between speaking now or being vindicated at considerable cost later. The cost of silence is not paid in the meeting; it is paid across the months that follow.

- [`surface_the_iceberg.md`](surface_the_iceberg.md) — Prepare the argument for surfacing hidden complexity before a room commits, including the most likely objection and how to pre-empt it.
