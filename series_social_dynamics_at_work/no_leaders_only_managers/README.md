# No Leaders, Only Managers

Prompts from this article in the Social Dynamics at Work series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_social_dynamics_at_work/no_leaders_only_managers](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_social_dynamics_at_work/no_leaders_only_managers)

The article describes the gradual, undramatic transformation from leader to manager — not through a single surrender, but through an accumulation of small adjustments that each felt like pragmatism. This prompt examines whether the sum of those adjustments represents growth or drift.

## Prompts

- [`audit_cumulative_drift.md`](audit_cumulative_drift.md) — Examine the pattern of professional adjustments over time to distinguish healthy adaptation from gradual erosion of core commitments.
