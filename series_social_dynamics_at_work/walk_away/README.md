# Walk Away

Prompts from this article in the Social Dynamics at Work series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_social_dynamics_at_work/walk_away](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_social_dynamics_at_work/walk_away)

The article describes the rescue that should not have been made — and the lesson that took too long to learn: do not rescue systems that have not earned the rescue. Every rescue removes the consequence that is the only teacher the system will accept.

- [`evaluate_the_rescue.md`](evaluate_the_rescue.md) — Trace the pattern of how a crisis came to exist and identify who is insulated from the consequences of their decisions if you resolve it.
