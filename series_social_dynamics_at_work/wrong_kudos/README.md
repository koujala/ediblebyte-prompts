# Wrong Kudos

Prompts from this article in the Social Dynamics at Work series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_social_dynamics_at_work/wrong_kudos](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_social_dynamics_at_work/wrong_kudos)

The article traces what happens after recognition lands at the wrong address — the quiet, rational recalibration by high performers who adjust their effort toward the thing that produced the recognition rather than the thing that produced the result. Recognition systems teach people what the organisation actually values, not what it says it values.
