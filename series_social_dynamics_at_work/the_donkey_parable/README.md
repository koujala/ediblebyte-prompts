# The Donkey Parable — No Configuration of Your Choices Will Silence the Crowd

Prompts from this article in the Social Dynamics at Work series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_social_dynamics_at_work/the_donkey_parable](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_social_dynamics_at_work/the_donkey_parable)

The article retells the Aesop fable as a professional lesson: there is no configuration of your choices that silences the crowd, because the commentary was never about the donkey — it is about people with no stake in your journey spending opinions they didn't have to earn. This prompt replaces the crowd with an agenda-free challenge.

## Prompts

- [`signal_from_noise_decision_check.md`](signal_from_noise_decision_check.md) — Surface real structural weaknesses in a decision before the crowd gets involved, using a challenge that has no agenda and no stake in the outcome.
