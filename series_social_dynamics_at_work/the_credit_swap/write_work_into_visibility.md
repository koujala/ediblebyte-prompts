# Write Your Work Into Visibility Before the Presentation

Use this prompt to create a clear, specific, ownable record of work you completed — before it goes into a meeting, a deck, or a handover. The goal is attribution that exists in the record before the presentation moment, not a dispute after.

---

```
I completed [DESCRIBE THE WORK — the analysis, the strategy, the solution, the decision].

I need to make this clearly and professionally attributable to me before it goes into a presentation. Help me write a one-page summary that captures what I built, the approach I took, and the key decisions I made. It should be specific enough that anyone reading it knows exactly whose thinking this is.
```

---

**How to use:**

Be concrete in the description — "I ran the numbers" is not specific enough. "I built the go-to-market model, sized three segments, and recommended killing the enterprise tier based on CAC-to-LTV analysis" is. The more specific the input, the harder the output is to attribute to someone else.

Send or circulate the document before the meeting where the work will be presented. A summary with your name and a timestamp that predates the meeting creates a record the presentation moment cannot erase.

This is not being litigious. It is existing in the record. The person who does not have a document does not exist in the record — regardless of what they actually built.
