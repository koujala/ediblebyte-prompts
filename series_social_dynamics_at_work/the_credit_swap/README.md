# The Credit Swap

Prompts from this article in the Social Dynamics at Work series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_social_dynamics_at_work/the_credit_swap](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_social_dynamics_at_work/the_credit_swap)

The article examines how attribution erodes without malice — through proximity bias, Fundamental Attribution Error, and the simple fact that the person who presents the work often gets credited for it. AI does not take credit, but it can help you build the paper trail that makes attribution hard to erase.

## Prompts

- [`write_work_into_visibility.md`](write_work_into_visibility.md) — Write a clear, ownable, pre-presentation summary of work you completed so attribution is established before the room meets.
- [`narrate_contribution_without_grievance.md`](narrate_contribution_without_grievance.md) — Frame your contribution factually and specifically in a review conversation without sounding like a complaint.
