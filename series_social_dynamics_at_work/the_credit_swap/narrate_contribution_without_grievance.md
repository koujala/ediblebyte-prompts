# Narrate Your Contribution Without Making a Grievance

Use this prompt to raise your visibility for a specific piece of work in a conversation with a manager or stakeholder — in a way that is factual and specific without sounding like you are complaining about credit.

---

```
I want to raise my visibility for [SPECIFIC PIECE OF WORK] with [MANAGER/STAKEHOLDER — their role and your relationship to them].

I am not comfortable self-promoting. Help me frame what I contributed in a way that is factual and specific without sounding like I am complaining about credit. I want to make the work legible, not make a grievance.
```

---

**How to use:**

The distinction the prompt is drawing is between two registers:
- **Grievance:** "I did all the work and didn't get credit"
- **Legibility:** "I want you to understand what I built and the decisions I made"

The second is professional, useful to the listener, and does not create adversarial dynamics. The AI will help you find language in that register.

Bring specific examples of what you actually did — the decisions made, the problems solved, the output produced. The more concrete the contribution, the less the framing sounds like a claim and the more it sounds like a briefing.
