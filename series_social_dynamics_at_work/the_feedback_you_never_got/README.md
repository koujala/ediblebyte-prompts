# The Feedback You Never Got

Prompts from this article in the Social Dynamics at Work series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_social_dynamics_at_work/the_feedback_you_never_got](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_social_dynamics_at_work/the_feedback_you_never_got)

The article argues that most professionals have never received real feedback — the kind that names the gap between self-perception and how they actually land. Managers avoid it to prevent conflict. Peers avoid it to protect relationships. AI has no relationship to protect, which makes it uniquely suited to give the honest evaluation most people have never received.

## Prompts

- [`honest_gap_analysis.md`](honest_gap_analysis.md) — Get a direct evaluation of a piece of work or communication that focuses on what is missing and what lands poorly, not what works.
