# The Meeting After the Meeting

Prompts from this article in the Social Dynamics at Work series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_social_dynamics_at_work/the_meeting_after_the_meeting](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_social_dynamics_at_work/the_meeting_after_the_meeting)

The article describes how formal meetings are often the performance of decision-making, while the actual decision was made in a corridor conversation among people with sufficient proximity to the decision-maker to be trusted with the informal version. AI removes the dependency on corridor access by helping you build a case that survives outside the informal room.

## Prompts

- [`corridor_proof_proposal.md`](corridor_proof_proposal.md) — Rewrite a proposal that has not gained traction as a pre-read that forces the decision into the formal record.
- [`hallway_hook.md`](hallway_hook.md) — Compress a proposal into one to three sentences that make a decision-maker curious enough to engage in sixty seconds.
