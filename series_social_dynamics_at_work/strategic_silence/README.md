# Strategic Silence — When Not Speaking Is the Sharpest Move in the Room

Prompts from this article in the Social Dynamics at Work series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_social_dynamics_at_work/strategic_silence](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_social_dynamics_at_work/strategic_silence)

The article argues that silence is a deployable skill — not absence or hesitation — that most professionals never develop because every career incentive pushes toward speaking. The person who speaks first after an offer is made almost always concedes ground. This prompt builds that muscle safely.
