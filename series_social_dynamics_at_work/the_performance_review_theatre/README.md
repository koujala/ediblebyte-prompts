# The Performance Review Theatre

Prompts from this article in the Social Dynamics at Work series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_social_dynamics_at_work/the_performance_review_theatre](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_social_dynamics_at_work/the_performance_review_theatre)

The article describes three biases that converge in annual reviews — recency bias, halo effect, and Goodhart's Law — and how they systematically reward visible performance over actual impact. The review is not measuring the year; it is measuring the relationship with the reviewer, filtered through the last quarter.

## Prompts

- [`build_contribution_record.md`](build_contribution_record.md) — Turn a rough list of year-long work into specific, evidence-based contribution bullets that can survive the review cycle.
- [`structure_the_review_conversation.md`](structure_the_review_conversation.md) — Frame a strong full-year record against a difficult recent quarter without sounding defensive.
