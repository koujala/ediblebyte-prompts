# Feedback vs. Commentary — Not Everyone Who Has an Opinion Has Earned the Right to Give One

Prompts from this article in the Social Dynamics at Work series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_social_dynamics_at_work/feedback_vs_commentary](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_social_dynamics_at_work/feedback_vs_commentary)

The article draws a sharp distinction between feedback — which comes from someone with domain knowledge, skin in the game, and genuine investment in your outcome — and commentary, which costs the giver nothing and is available in unlimited supply. The source determines the weight, not the content.
