# First AI Hire — JD and Interview Design

Design the job description, screening criteria, and interview process for a bilingual first AI hire — technically capable and able to navigate procurement, legal, and business stakeholders.

```
We are hiring our first dedicated AI role. Here is our current AI maturity, our key internal stakeholders, and our deployment barriers: [describe]. Help me design the job description, screening criteria, and interview process for a bilingual AI hire — not just technically capable, but organisationally effective.
```

**When to use:** Before writing the JD for your first dedicated AI hire. A technically brilliant first hire who can't navigate the organisation makes every subsequent hire harder.
