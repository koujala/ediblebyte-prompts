# The Summary Shortcut

Prompts from the **Summary Shortcut** article in the Learning Traps series.  
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_learning_traps/the_summary_shortcut](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_learning_traps/the_summary_shortcut)

The article argues that the friction of reading something slowly — losing the thread, rereading, not quite understanding, sitting with the confusion — was not an obstacle to the learning. It was the mechanism by which the learning happened. The summary delivers the conclusion without the struggle. And the struggle was where the understanding was being built.

## Prompts

- [`recreate_the_friction.md`](recreate_the_friction.md) — Deliberately recreate the cognitive work a summary bypassed, by working out the concept from a scenario rather than being told it.
