# The Spaced Repetition Gap

Prompts from the **Spaced Repetition Gap** article in the Learning Traps series.  
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_learning_traps/the_spaced_repetition_gap](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_learning_traps/the_spaced_repetition_gap)

The article names the Forgetting Curve — documented by Ebbinghaus in 1885 and replicated ever since. Without reinforcement, humans lose approximately 70% of new information within 24 hours and over 90% within a week. The countermeasure, spaced repetition at increasing intervals, has been known for 140 years. Most educational institutions still ignore it entirely. The science has been clear. The operational cost of implementing it has been the barrier. AI eliminates that barrier.

## Prompts

- [`design_a_spaced_repetition_schedule.md`](design_a_spaced_repetition_schedule.md) — Build a month-long retrieval schedule for key concepts, with questions that require production rather than recognition.
- [`turn_a_lesson_into_durable_knowledge.md`](turn_a_lesson_into_durable_knowledge.md) — Lock in what you just learned before the Forgetting Curve takes hold — retrieval questions and a review structure, right now.
