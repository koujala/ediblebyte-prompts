# The Sunk Cost Classroom

Prompts from the **Sunk Cost Classroom** article in the Learning Traps series.  
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_learning_traps/the_sunk_cost_classroom](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_learning_traps/the_sunk_cost_classroom)

The article names the sunk cost fallacy applied to learning paths and career commitments. The past cost is gone — it left the moment it was spent. The honest question is never "how much have I already invested?" but "if I were starting today, knowing what I know now, would I choose this?" Continuing to pay for a sunk cost with future time and opportunity is not discipline — it's the mind refusing to update.

## Prompts

- [`evaluate_as_day_one.md`](evaluate_as_day_one.md) — Remove the sunk cost from the evaluation by asking AI to assess the path forward as if the decision is being made for the first time today.
