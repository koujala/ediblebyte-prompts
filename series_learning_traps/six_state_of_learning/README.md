# The Six Stages of Learning

Prompts from the **Six Stages of Learning** article in the Learning Traps series.  
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_learning_traps/six_state_of_learning](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_learning_traps/six_state_of_learning)

The article maps six stages of learning — from Unaware through Creative Mastery — and names the trap: Stage 2 (Aware) feels identical to Stage 4 (Proficient). Untested confidence looks exactly like earned confidence, and most people stop moving at Stage 2 without knowing it.

## Prompts

- [`stress_test_your_stage.md`](stress_test_your_stage.md) — Find out what a practitioner two stages ahead would challenge you on, and which parts of your knowledge are theoretical rather than tested.
