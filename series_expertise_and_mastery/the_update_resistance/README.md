# The Update Resistance — Prompts

Prompts from this article in the Expertise and Mastery series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_expertise_and_mastery/the_update_resistance](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_expertise_and_mastery/the_update_resistance)

The article's diagnosis: deep expertise creates identity investment in a mental model — which makes that model the hardest one to revise. AI has a structural advantage here: it steelmans the opposing view without ego investment, political stakes, or interpersonal history. Use it to engage with the argument on its merits rather than filtering it through who is making it.

## Prompts

- [`steelman_the_view_you_resist.md`](steelman_the_view_you_resist.md) — Get the strongest possible case for the idea or change you have been resisting, without softening or acknowledging your objections.
- [`audit_your_mental_model.md`](audit_your_mental_model.md) — Test whether the core assumptions your expertise is built on are still current.
