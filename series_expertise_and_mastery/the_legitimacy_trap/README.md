# The Legitimacy Trap — Prompts

Prompts from this article in the Expertise and Mastery series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_expertise_and_mastery/the_legitimacy_trap](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_expertise_and_mastery/the_legitimacy_trap)

The article's diagnosis: impostor syndrome is most prevalent among the genuinely capable. The more you know, the more clearly you see what you do not know — and institutional credentials and actual competence are different axes. The prompts here build two things: the evidence that bypasses legitimacy anxiety, and the preparation that makes the anxiety irrelevant.

## Prompts

- [`build_track_record_evidence.md`](build_track_record_evidence.md) — Turn specific work, decisions, and outcomes into a fair-minded case for your expertise — no credentials required.
- [`prepare_past_the_confidence_problem.md`](prepare_past_the_confidence_problem.md) — Stress-test content until preparation is not the issue, leaving only the doing.
