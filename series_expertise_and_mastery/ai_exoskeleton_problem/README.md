# AI is an Exoskeleton — Prompts

Prompts from this article in the Expertise and Mastery series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_expertise_and_mastery/ai_exoskeleton_problem](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_expertise_and_mastery/ai_exoskeleton_problem)

The article's central test: can you defend your AI-assisted output without the AI? If not, you are wearing the exoskeleton rather than building the muscle underneath. The prompt here switches the AI from ghostwriter to sparring partner — it challenges your reasoning instead of supplying it.

## Prompts

- [`sparring_partner_not_ghostwriter.md`](sparring_partner_not_ghostwriter.md) — Switch AI from output-generator to challenger so you build the underlying competence, not just the result.
