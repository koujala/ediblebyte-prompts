# Sparring Partner, Not Ghostwriter

Use this when you have been leaning on AI to produce output in a domain you want to genuinely own. The prompt strips AI of the leading role and puts you in it — you do the thinking, AI challenges it. This is how AI builds competence rather than substituting for it.

```
I've been using AI to help with [TASK — e.g. writing architecture proposals / drafting analyses / solving data problems].

Now work with me without leading. Ask me questions, challenge my reasoning, let me do the work. Stop me if I'm just confirming what I already said.

Start with: ask me to explain [SPECIFIC ASPECT OF THE TASK] in my own words, then push back on anything that sounds borrowed rather than understood.
```

**How to use the output:** If you can hold your ground under the AI's challenge — explaining, defending, revising — you have the underlying competence. If you find yourself pulling from things the AI previously told you without being able to reconstruct the reasoning, that is the exoskeleton doing the work. Repeat until you can carry the reasoning yourself.
