# The Mentor You Idealized — Prompts

Prompts from this article in the Expertise and Mastery series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_expertise_and_mastery/the_mentor_you_idealized](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_expertise_and_mastery/the_mentor_you_idealized)

The article's diagnosis: waiting for the idealized mentor is often waiting for permission, not readiness. The readiness was never going to come from outside. The prompt here uses AI not to validate whether a step is safe, but to map exactly what the step requires — separating the genuine obstacles from the permission-seeking.

- [`map_the_next_step.md`](map_the_next_step.md) — Diagnose whether readiness is the actual issue or whether you are waiting for permission that is not coming.
