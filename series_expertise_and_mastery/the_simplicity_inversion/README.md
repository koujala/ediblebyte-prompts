# The Simplicity Inversion — Prompts

Prompts from this article in the Expertise and Mastery series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_expertise_and_mastery/the_simplicity_inversion](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_expertise_and_mastery/the_simplicity_inversion)

The article's diagnosis: the Curse of Knowledge makes deep expertise harder to communicate simply. The expert who communicates at the complexity level of their acquisition loses the room. True mastery is the ability to say the essential thing and trust that the depth exists if anyone asks. These prompts build the skill of moving between the two.

## Prompts

- [`distill_to_essential.md`](distill_to_essential.md) — Compress deep knowledge into a one-sentence recommendation before walking into the room.
- [`curse_of_knowledge_test.md`](curse_of_knowledge_test.md) — Find exactly where your explanation stops being accessible to someone without your background.
