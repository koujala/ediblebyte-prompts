# What the Dreyfus Brothers Got Right — Prompts

Prompts from this article in the Expertise and Mastery series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_expertise_and_mastery/education_dreyfus_brothers_expertise](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_expertise_and_mastery/education_dreyfus_brothers_expertise)

The article maps skill from Stage 1 (rules as safety) to Stage 5 (rules dissolved into instinct). The key insight for practice: the move from Stage 3 to Stage 4 requires deliberately encountering situations where rules produce the wrong outcome — forcing judgment rather than procedure. The prompt here creates those situations.

## Prompts

- [`rule_breaking_scenario.md`](rule_breaking_scenario.md) — Generate situations where following the rules exactly produces the wrong outcome, to practise judgment over procedure.
