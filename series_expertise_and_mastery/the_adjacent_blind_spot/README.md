# The Adjacent Blind Spot — Prompts

Prompts from this article in the Expertise and Mastery series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_expertise_and_mastery/the_adjacent_blind_spot](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_expertise_and_mastery/the_adjacent_blind_spot)

The article's diagnosis: deep expertise in one domain produces elevated confidence in adjacent ones — without the epistemics to know where that confidence is justified. The prompts here provide a consistent cross-domain scanner before a decision is made, and a structured way to approach adjacent areas with genuine beginner's mind rather than borrowed confidence.

## Prompts

- [`scan_adjacent_blind_spots.md`](scan_adjacent_blind_spots.md) — Stress-test a decision across the domains you may not be seeing clearly before proceeding.
- [`beginners_mind_in_adjacent_domain.md`](beginners_mind_in_adjacent_domain.md) — Enter an adjacent area with explicit beginner's framing rather than confidence borrowed from your main expertise.
