# Expert Edge Check

Use this when you feel certain about an answer in your domain. The point is not to find out what you got wrong — it is to find out what you have stopped asking. Expertise builds pattern recognition that is powerful and also quietly filters out questions that feel beneath the level of the title. This prompt reopens those questions.

```
I'm going to answer [QUESTION IN YOUR DOMAIN — e.g. the right approach to microservices at scale / how to structure a performance review conversation / when to rewrite vs. refactor].

[GIVE YOUR ANSWER AT YOUR NORMAL DEPTH]

Now tell me what a genuine expert at the edge of this field would add, challenge, or reframe — not what I got wrong, but what I haven't yet considered. I want the questions and framings that only surface when you have gone further into this than most people go.
```

**How to use the output:** The value is not in whether your answer was correct — it probably was, within its frame. The value is in the additional frame. If you can sit with the AI's reframing without immediately reaching for the override, you are practising beginner's mind inside expertise. If you feel the reflex to dismiss it, that reflex is worth examining.
