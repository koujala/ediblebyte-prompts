# The Expert Trap — Prompts

Prompts from this article in the Expertise and Mastery series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_expertise_and_mastery/the_expert_trap](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_expertise_and_mastery/the_expert_trap)

The trap is expertise that has stopped being curious — certainty that feels like competence but is quietly closing off inquiry. The article's practice: ask AI a question you think you already know the answer to, then attend to what you haven't yet considered, not what you got wrong.

## Prompts

- [`expert_edge_check.md`](expert_edge_check.md) — Surface what a genuine expert at the edge of the field would add, challenge, or reframe — not what you got wrong, but what you haven't yet considered.
