# Social Learning Gone Wrong — Prompts

Prompts from this article in the Expertise and Mastery series.
Read the full article: [ediblebyte.com/publications/series_expertise_and_mastery/social_learning_gone_wrong](https://www.ediblebyte.com/publications/series_expertise_and_mastery/social_learning_gone_wrong)

The article's diagnosis: "everyone knows that" is where unexamined inherited beliefs live. Social learning is efficient in stable domains and quietly expensive in fast-moving ones. The prompt here uses AI to steelman the opposite — not to be contrarian, but to find out whether the belief survives examination or was only ever surviving by consensus.

## Prompts

- [`steelman_the_opposite.md`](steelman_the_opposite.md) — Challenge a received belief in your field by asking AI to make the strongest possible case against it.
