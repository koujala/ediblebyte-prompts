# Steelman the Opposite

Use this when you encounter a belief in your field that is treated as settled — the kind that circulates as received wisdom rather than tested conclusion. The goal is not to demolish it but to subject it to the strongest available challenge. Beliefs that survive a real steelman are beliefs you can hold with confidence. The ones that don't survive were running your strategy on consensus alone.

```
Everyone in my field assumes [BELIEF — e.g. large teams ship complex products better than small ones / this regulatory approach is the safe one / this technology is past its peak].

Steelman the opposite as strongly as possible — don't soften it. Give me 5 key points that challenge this belief, each with a brief explanation of the reasoning or evidence behind it. I want to know if this belief survives a real challenge or was only surviving by consensus.
```

**How to use the output:** For each point the AI raises, ask yourself: have I actually encountered this argument before, or was I operating without it? If the belief holds after reading all five points, you now hold it with evidence. If one or more points land — that is the belief quietly running your decisions that you did not know was untested.
