# RAG vs Fine-Tuning Decision

Use when making your first AI customization decision — whether your gap is a knowledge problem (RAG) or a behavior problem (fine-tuning).

```
We are trying to solve this AI gap: [describe what the model gets wrong today]. Our knowledge assets are: [describe docs, policies, data]. Our behavior requirements are: [describe tone, format, compliance constraints]. Should we use RAG, fine-tuning, or both? Walk me through the decision.
```

**When to use:** Before committing compute budget and team time to either path. The most expensive mistake is answering the wrong question.
